ⓘ  How to Open Your Group Calendar in Outlook
1
Open Outlook and go to the Calendar view (click the calendar icon at the bottom-left).
2
In the left sidebar under "Other calendars" or "Shared Calendars", look for your group calendar name shown above (e.g. Engineering).
3
If you don't see your group calendar, click the ellipsis icon (…) → Folders → All Public Folders → find your calendar name → right-click and Add to Favorites.
4
Add your event using the format: Chuck Konkol – OOO or Chuck Konkol – Working Elsewhere or Chuck Konkol – Offsite.
